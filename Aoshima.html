Aoshima Shokudo: Ginger Shoyu

Rocation: Akihabara, Tokyo, JP

Shoyu is what we generally called soy sauce or ponzu. But, it is must bigger category in Japan

Aoshima's shoyu is aged with ginger and taste as ginger

For the topping, it comes with bambu shoots, bean sprout, spinach, seaweed, spring anion, and pork slices

It was the first meal after I arrived Japan in January of 2025. It is really famous place, and i have to wait about 50 min to get into the restaurant. It was worthy to wait 50min. Highly recommanded
