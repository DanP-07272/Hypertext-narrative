566: Jiro kei

Location: Hongdae, Seoul, S.Korea

Jiro kei is pretty special type of ramen because the name is not from the ingredients, but from name of the resporant

Jiro kei is well known as high calorie, massive amount of noodle and topping, and strong flavored tonkotsu soup

Because it is such a strong experience, it became so popular in Japan in early 2000s, and now it even exist outside of Japan

I went this place right after I ended my journey in Japan. And it tastes just like Japan
