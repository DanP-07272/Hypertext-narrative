Hirakoya: Niboshi

Location: Tokyo Station, Tokyo, JP

Niboshi is, unlike other ramens, not based on chicken or pork based soup. But, the soup is based on anchovy.

Hirakoya's Niboshi has extremely strong flavor soup, and you can taste the flavor of anchovy straight forward.

The fact that there is not that many Niboshi place in NYC(or even entire United States) is so sad, especially for ramen lovers like me.

It is new, something you cannot experience in New York easily,
