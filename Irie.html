Irie: Cod based white shio

Location: Hongdae, Seoul, S.Korea

Just like Niboshi(anchovy based ramen), there's few ramen that is based on seafood instead of meat based soup.

Here is one of the very unique place because it is really hard to find cod based soup, especially outside of Japan.

Unlike Niboshi, it doesn't taste stong. In fact, the flavor is really soft and fresh, almost cannot taste fish.
